## SLIDE 4: THE SHIP OF THESEUS - PHILOSOPHY

"This is where it gets philosophical.

There's an ancient paradox called the Ship of Theseus. It asks: 'If you replace every part of a ship, is it still the same ship?'

We applied this to drug discovery.

**Here's how it works:**

Start with Aspirin. We know Aspirin is safe. We've given it to billions of people. It works.

But what if we evolved it?

Generation 1: We change 5% of its atoms. It's 95% Aspirin still.
Generation 2: We change 15% of its atoms. It's 85% Aspirin now.
Generation 3: We change 40% of its atoms. It's 60% Aspirin.
Generation 4: We change 65% of its atoms. It's 35% Aspirin.
Generation 5: We change 90% of its atoms. It's only 10% Aspirin.

**At this point, legally, it's a NEW DRUG.**

You can patent it. You can patent it as an NCE - New Chemical Entity.

But here's the beautiful part: It's descended from Aspirin. It started with something proven safe. But it's something completely new.

That's the Ship of Theseus applied to drug discovery."
